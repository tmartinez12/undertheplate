About the Project & Our Philosophy

Under the Plate is a community cookbook and a growing series of food gatherings. We created this space to bring together recipes, flavors, and memories that reconnect us with the earth.

For us, cooking is never just about nourishment it is an act of love. Every recipe we share carries a story, every flavor holds a memory, and together they remind us of our bond with nature, with one another, and with ourselves.

What matters most to us is the exchange: sharing recipes with people from different places and backgrounds. We believe that food is one of the most precious forms of knowledge we can pass on. Each dish carries wisdom, tradition, and care treasures that become even richer when shared.

Our values

Connection

Food unites us across cultures, generations, and distances.

Nature

Fruits and vegetables and everything that nourish, are more than ingredients; they are gifts from the earth.

Stories

Every recipe tells where we come from, who we are, and what we cherish.

Love

Cooking is a gesture of care, a way to nourish not just the body but also the soul.

Our Under the Plate Gathering

Our first gathering took place at Fòla, in Milan, in April. We came together with some of the people who share their recipes with us, around one table, to talk about what's really under the plate.

We spoke about where our food comes from the stories behind the ingredients we love, and how far they've traveled to reach us. And we spoke about what we might lose. Climate change is putting some of our favorite drinks at risk.

It was a simple evening, but it stayed with us. Food is never just food it's memory, it's connection. Thank you to everyone who pulled up a chair.
